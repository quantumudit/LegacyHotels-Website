About Legacy Hotels

Legacy Hotels is a prestigious hotel chain that offers unparalleled hospitality and comfort to its guests. Founded in 1970, Legacy Hotels has a rich history of serving the elite travelers who visit Kolkata, the cultural capital of India. Kolkata has a long and fascinating history as a British settlement since 1690, and is home to many historical monuments, museums, temples and markets.

Legacy Hotels has four properties in Kolkata, each with its own distinctive style and charm. Whether you are looking for a colonial elegance at The Lalit Great Eastern Kolkata, a contemporary luxury at ITC Sonar Kolkata A Luxury Collection Hotel , a heritage grandeur at The Oberoi Grand Kolkata or a modern sophistication at JW Marriott Hotel Kolkata, you will find your perfect stay at Legacy Hotels.
